Level Design: Super Mario Maker
Sometimes Super Mario Maker level-makers like to play tricks on the player to add a bit of life to a difficult puzzle or platforming challenge. These tricks are referred to in the SMM community as "trolls", and levels that are built entirely out of these tricks, repeatedly anticipating and subverting the player's expectations, are known as "troll levels".

Making a good troll level requires finesse to ensure that the trolls are effective, the deception is entertaining, and the player has enough information to continue through the level once they have been "trolled".

These elements make playtesting and revisions a crucial part of the process, and reward a strong understanding of the player's thought process.
